How We Operate

Patience over speed. Receipts, not theater.

Our edge is patience. We refuse to publish until the setup earns it. What makes it past the filter goes on the dashboard, live, every time. Win or lose, the receipts stay public.

Principle 1

Patience over speed

We do not chase. The signals that make it through come from setups where the conviction is real, not retail noise. Most days produce zero new entries, and that is on purpose.

Principle 2

Cross-check the broader picture

Setups that fight the broader environment do not get published. The trade has to make sense in the context of what the rest of the tape is doing.

Principle 3

Most candidates die quietly

The bar is high. The vast majority of what we look at gets tracked internally or discarded. Subscribers see only the setups that earn publication. No filler.

Principle 4

Track every published signal live

Entry captured at signal fire with real option chain pricing. Peak and current updated continuously during market hours. No retroactive edits.

Principle 5

Close only at calendar expiry

A signal closes publicly when its option contract expires, not when we feel like it. The -50% display toggle on the dashboard shows the conservative view for readers who want it; it does not close positions.

Principle 6

Show every win and every loss

Peak % is the signal-quality metric. Win = peak reached at least +20%. Loss = peak never reached the threshold. Losses are not hidden. That is the whole point.
