Why CoreWeave

CoreWeave operates GPU cloud rental capacity dedicated to LLM training and inference. Microsoft and Meta are anchor customers. Nvidia is both supplier and equity stakeholder. The structural picture is irreplaceable scarcity: if Nvidia GPU allocation flows to anyone first, it flows here.

On April 9 the stock was around $90. The flow signature surfaced late in the session: multiple institutional buy-blocks on the May call chain, ask-side dollar share at 78% on the print, and CoreWeave appearing in the top-10 by dollar volume on the dark pool tape. That last point matters a lot for a sub-$30B-cap name. Big names don't usually appear there unless somebody is taking a position.

The contract choice

The $110 call May 8 flagged at $4.00/contract. About 22% out-of-the-money on a 4-week expiry. Cheap enough to size, far enough out-of-the-money that a real institutional move would expand the premium meaningfully if the structural picture confirmed.
