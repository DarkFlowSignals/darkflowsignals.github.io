My pick: V2 (trajectory arrow). Peak still leads as the headline (signal quality), but the trajectory line "now ▼ +254% (-152pp)" answers a real member question — "where is it now relative to its best moment?" — without burying the peak. V1 is the safe minimal choice; V3 is the most distinctive but the pulsing dot scales poorly.
