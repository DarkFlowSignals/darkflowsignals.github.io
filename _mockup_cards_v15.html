What v15 ships:
  • Same row structure on desktop AND mobile — only typography sizes change. Cleaner mental model, easier to maintain.
  • Sparkline becomes a full-width hero on desktop (1000px viewBox stretching the card width, 60px tall). On mobile it shrinks but still spans full width.
  • Every metric-bearing row uses space-between — header has peak on right, conviction has status on right, bottom has entered on right. Card uses both edges.
  • Spot and option/lifecycle stay full-width — they don't have right-side metrics, so they read across the card naturally.
  • Expanded panel is the v6/v13 design carried over: pricing arrow, Greeks, earnings, full 4-pillar thesis (Setup / Catalyst / Macro / Risk), cohort base rate, TradingView chart. Each section in its own surface card with consistent labeling.
