# How to report
# - Email security@darkflowsignals.com (or rgoguen18@gmail.com) with details.
# - Please include reproduction steps and impact assessment.
# - We aim to acknowledge within 48 hours and remediate critical findings within 14 days.
# - We do not currently run a paid bug bounty, but credit will be given on the response page.

# Out of scope
# - Self-signed certificate or cipher-suite reports without practical exploit context.
# - Volumetric DDoS reports (handled by Cloudflare).
# - Social engineering of employees / contractors.
