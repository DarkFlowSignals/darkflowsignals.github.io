What changed in v7:
  • Removed footer entirely — no more "Cohort: n=11 · won 73%" line + no more "↓ click for full detail" line. Cohort lives in the expand panel; click affordance is now the chevron.
  • Subtle chevron `›` in the top-right corner. Muted gray by default, animates blue + nudges right on hover. Clean way to signal "click me" without a visual line.
  • Sparkline got bigger in both layouts (38px → 70px in A, full width × 80px in B).
  • Mini axis labels under the sparkline show entry / peak / current premium dollars at a glance — no click needed for the basic price journey.
  • Layout A: 240px right rail (was 200px). Sparkline anchors that rail vertically.
  • Layout B: Full-width chart at the bottom. More dramatic visual presence; card looks like a Bloomberg-style data card.

My pick: Layout A. Right-rail sparkline keeps the card horizontally compact (less vertical scroll for members with 7+ open positions), the 70px chart is big enough to read the journey, and the axis labels add real info without bloat. Layout B is more dramatic but takes ~50% more vertical space per card.
